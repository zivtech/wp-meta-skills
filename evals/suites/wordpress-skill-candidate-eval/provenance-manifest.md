# Provenance Manifest

Fixtures and rubrics are synthetic and generated for the Zivtech WordPress V1 candidate evaluation. They are based on the local plan, the 2026-06-16 upstream candidate catalog, the rendered skills.sh website survey at `https://www.skills.sh/?q=wordpress`, and WordPress domain coverage needs. They do not copy upstream skill text.

Candidate sources are tracked in `wordpress-skills/docs/candidate-catalog.md`.
